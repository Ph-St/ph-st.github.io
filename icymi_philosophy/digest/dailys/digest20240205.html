Proposed thesis about Art:

If something is sensorial (as with music, painting, etc.) or imaginary (as with fiction, poetry, etc.) and it makes you either ask questions or create concepts, it can be art (it's aesthetic; whether it's art also hangs on whether it's of intentional vs. accidental origin).

If it doesn't cause you to have a question or idea, then it is not art.
