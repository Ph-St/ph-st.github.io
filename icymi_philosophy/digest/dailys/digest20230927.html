One of the 'false friends' in Dutch and English that it took me longest to recognise is 'cursive' versus 'cursief'. Both pertain to (hand)writing; and there are not a lot of common contexts where you can find you that you have a slightly mistaken understanding of what 'cursive' means. But Dutch 'cursief' means what in English is called 'italic', and of course that's not what English 'cursive' means, which is the name for styles of handwriting that connect all the letters.
