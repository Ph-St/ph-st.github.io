Selfish plutocrats
Wish they'd live 'til end of time.
Reckless, seems they will.

#haiku #senryu #poem #ShortPoem #SmallPoem #ethics #futurism #collapse #life #tech #technology #wisdom #mortality #lifespan #extinction

Ben Franklin said (in 1780): "I have sometimes almost wished it had been my destiny to be born two or three centuries hence. For invention and improvement are prolific, and progress in them seems to be accelerating. What prodigious improvements may we not expect in a few years?"

I sometimes feel as if the world's tech giant billionaires think this same, but are lulled by their riches into a false belief that they can bend space and time to bring that future into the present, against the advice of Star Trek, which many times reminds us that when knowledge outpaces wisdom, disaster looms.

We can rush tech, but wisdom not so much. By rushing, we seem probably to be ending humanity's hope of having any future whatsoever. Collapse of civilization, quite possibly extinction, looms. Though this means those reckless, impatient, spoiled billionaires will not have missed out on seeing future human inventions. So... mission accomplished? They have, after all, lived to see it all.
